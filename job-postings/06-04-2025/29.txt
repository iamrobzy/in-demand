Job Details:

We are actively seeking a Machine Learning Engineer with Top Secret or SCI clearance to join our highly skilled team. This is an opportunity to work on high-impact, top-secret projects involving advanced machine learning and artificial intelligence. The ideal candidate will have a deep understanding of image and signal processing, RADAR, SAR (Synthetic Aperture Radar), and programming languages such as C++, C/C++, and Python. Knowledge of machine learning frameworks like Tensorflow and Keras is also essential.

Responsibilities:

Develop, implement, and optimize cutting-edge machine learning algorithms related to image processing, signal processing, RADAR, and Synthetic Aperture Radar (SAR).
Work closely with the data science team to design and implement models and algorithms that address complex business requirements.

Present complex findings in a clear and concise manner to non-technical stakeholders.
Continuously improve machine learning models, data collection and processing procedures to adapt to ever-changing defense requirements and technologies.

Minimum 5 years of experience in a similar role, with a focus on machine learning, image processing, signal processing, RADAR, and Synthetic Aperture Radar (SAR).

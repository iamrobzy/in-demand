About The Role

CrowdStrike’s Data Science Machine Learning Operations and Response Team is seeking a Threat Analyst to focus on both proactive and reactive threat analysis. In this role, you will analyze malware and threat detections, investigate detection tickets, and enhance detection capability and efficiency. You will work closely with internal teams, including Technical Account Managers, Falcon Complete, Data Scientists, and the Malware Research Center, playing a crucial role in improving detection efficacy and managing false positives.

Responsibilities

Review current product detections to ensure alignment with company standards.
Perform tasks aimed at better management of false positive detections.
Analyze binary files to assess legitimacy.
Address internal inquiries and concerns regarding customer threat detections.

Skills And Qualifications

Basic Qualifications

Exposure and understanding of malware types and functionality.
Experience with reverse engineering malware or malware operations.
Proficiency in Python and knowledge of programming and scripting languages.
Understanding of binary file attributes such as imports/exports and packers.
Practical knowledge of research/collection skills and analytical methods.
Familiarity with operating systems and threat/risk management concepts.

Bonus Points

Experience in a Security Operations Center (SOC) or incident response environment.
Thorough understanding of Windows OS internals and the Windows API.
Knowledge of MacOS and/or Linux.
Familiarity with tools used in cyber intrusions.
Background in exploit and vulnerability analysis.
Proficiency in programming languages such as C, C++, Java, and assembly.
Experience with threat detection by machine learning.

Education

BA/BS or MA/MS degree or equivalent experience in Computer Science, Information Security, or a related field.

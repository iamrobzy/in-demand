The Enterprise Data Warehouse Machine Learning Engineering Team is at the forefront of driving data-driven innovation and operational excellence across MSCI’s business processes. This team is dedicated to developing, delivering, and maintaining advanced insights that enhance business quality and efficiency. By leveraging state-of-the-art techniques in data engineering, machine learning, AI, and business intelligence, the team supports various sectors including coverage, product management, and finance.

Team Level Key Responsibilities

Develop and maintain data-driven insights to optimize business processes and improve quality across MSCI.
Establish and enhance an analytical framework within the data lakehousing/warehousing center of excellence.
Maintain and refine machine learning and AI-focused data processing, augmentation, and insight generation pipelines.
Enhance the effectiveness of machine learning models and AI systems through feature engineering, model training, validation, decay analysis, prompt engineering, and AI agentic design.
Analyze and formalize business challenges, translating them into executable software projects.
Implement and deploy analytical solutions in production environments to facilitate data-driven decision-making.
Enhance the software development lifecycle through the application of DEVOPS and MLOPS methodologies.
Design and maintain distributed big data and AI computation environments in containerized and cloud settings.

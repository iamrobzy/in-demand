Key Responsibilities

Software Development & Automation

Design, develop, and maintain custom software applications for engineering workflows using C#.

Automate repetitive engineering tasks in MEP, CFD, and structural analysis to improve efficiency.

Develop algorithms to process and analyze engineering data, integrating AI models for predictive insights.

Machine Learning & AI Integration

Implement machine learning models for image recognition, pattern detection, and predictive analytics in engineering projects.

Utilize AI-powered tools to enhance Revit modeling, CFD analysis, and structural simulations.

Apply computer vision techniques to analyze engineering drawings, blueprints, and CAD models.

Engineering & Computational Analysis

Assist in the development of AI-driven CFD simulations for airflow, heat distribution, and energy efficiency predictions.

Enhance predictive modeling for HVAC, electrical, and plumbing systems to optimize design performance.

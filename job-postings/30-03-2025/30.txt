Job Details

We are seeking a passionate and highly skilled Permanent Health Equity BI Developer to join our dynamic Tech Services team. As a Health Equity BI Developer, you will play a critical role in developing and implementing BI solutions to improve health equity. This exciting role offers the opportunity to work at the intersection of tech and healthcare, using data to drive meaningful change and improve health outcomes for all.

Responsibilities

Develop, implement, and maintain BI solutions using tools such as Power BI, QLIK, and Azure SQL to improve health equity.
Design and build data models to support BI initiatives and ensure data accuracy and integrity.
Work closely with stakeholders to understand their needs and translate them into technical requirements.
Lead data integration efforts, ensuring data from various sources is accurately consolidated in the Data Warehouse.
Develop complex TSQL and SSIS packages for ETL processes.
Perform data analysis and create reports to support decision-making processes.
Collaborate with cross-functional teams to drive the implementation of new solutions.
Troubleshoot and resolve BI-related issues and provide technical support when needed.
Stay up-to-date with the latest industry trends and technologies to ensure our solutions are current and effective.

Qualifications

Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Information Systems, or a related field.
A minimum of 5 years of experience in a BI Developer role, preferably in the healthcare industry.
Proficiency in BI tools such as Power BI, QLIK, and Azure SQL.
Strong experience in data modeling, data integration, and working with Data Warehouses.
Proficiency in MSSQL, TSQL, and SSIS.
Experience in data analysis and report creation.
Excellent problem-solving skills and attention to detail.
Strong communication skills, with the ability to effectively communicate complex technical concepts to non-technical stakeholders.
Passion for health equity and a strong desire to use technology to improve health outcomes.
Ability to work effectively in a fast-paced, dynamic environment and manage multiple priorities.

This is a fantastic opportunity to leverage your technical skills to make a real difference in health equity. If you are passionate about data and healthcare and are ready to take on a challenging and rewarding role, we would love to hear from you.
